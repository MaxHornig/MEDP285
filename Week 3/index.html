    Biography

    I am a clinical researcher and patient representative with the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute, working with the RECOVER Initiative to diagnose and treat Long COVID. I have worked in laboratory research for the last 20 years, and until last year I taught a master's degree program in public health at Columbia University.

    Artist Statement


    I aim to create informative and engaging visual materials based on my social, academic, and clinical experience to explore the lived experience of dealing with and treating Long COVID and other post acute sequelae of infection. I aim to engage with the public on complex issues through simple and understandable means. As a queer individual with a chronic debilitating illness, one that may inevitably progress to my death, I feel compelled to share (through visual media) my story as well as the stories of others in my community. Learning the core elements of web design and production will help me succeed in engaging with the public. Over the course of this class I aim to:
    • Learn the essentials of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
    • Learn how to build engaging/function-focused websites
      • Learn the tools to present data and resources to those in need
      • Create a website that functions on desktop and mobile browsers with similar functionality
